## Key Visual Differences

### Typography
- **Before**: Generic fonts, mixed sizes
- **After**: Courier Prime (headings), IBM Plex Mono (body), consistent hierarchy

### Colors
- **Before**: Generic UI colors
- **After**: Warm wood tones, amber accents, cream backgrounds

### Textures
- **Before**: Flat colors
- **After**: Wood grain headers, paper texture cards, subtle grain overlay

### Components
- **Before**: Standard buttons and inputs
- **After**: Index card buttons, typewriter inputs, sticky note badges

### Layout
- **Before**: Dense, everything visible
- **After**: Spacious, progressive disclosure, clear hierarchy

### Feedback
- **Before**: Small, easy to miss
- **After**: Large stats, prominent warnings, clear progress
